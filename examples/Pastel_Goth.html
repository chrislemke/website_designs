Pastel Goth

where sugar skulls meet lavender dreams

A subcultural aesthetic fusing the soft, dreamy sweetness of pastel colors with the dark, macabre imagery of gothic subculture. Softness and darkness are not contradictions -- they are complements.

Typography

Creepy-Cute Letterforms

bubbly meets blackletter

Comfortaa Primary Display

Sweet Decay ☠

Rounded, wide, geometric. The primary display font for headlines and hero text. Its bubbly, approachable forms embody the kawaii side of Pastel Goth.

UnifrakturCook Gothic Accent

Darkness Eternal 🌘

Blackletter / Fraktur. Used sparingly for gothic accent headings, logos, and decorative text. Maximum contrast when paired with rounded body text.

Satisfy Script Accent

eternally yours, xo 🖤

Casual cursive script. Perfect for accent taglines, decorative quotes, and handwritten flourishes that add romantic gothic personality.

Fredoka Playful Bold

CREEPY CUTE! 💀

Bubbly, bold, playful. Great for sticker-style labels, button text, and call-to-action elements. Embodies the kawaii aesthetic.

Quicksand Body Text

The aesthetic's philosophy centers on duality: the acceptance that softness and darkness, fragility and fierceness, cuteness and creepiness are not contradictions but complements. In web design, this translates to dark backgrounds illuminated by pastel-colored elements.

Rounded geometric sans-serif. The primary body text font. Its soft letterforms ensure readability while maintaining the aesthetic's approachable character.

Permanent Marker Bangers Punk-Cute

grunge vibes

POW! 💥

dear diary... 💜

Handwritten, marker, and comic-book fonts for zine-inspired, DIY punk-cute accents. Use for rebellious emphasis, sticker labels, and diary-style annotations.
