Effects & Details

Liquid Motion and Visual Effects

Morphing SVGs, lava lamp blobs, water ripples, gooey metaballs, and melting transitions bring the fluid aesthetic to life.

SVG Morphing Blob

SMIL-animated SVG paths morph between organic shapes, creating complex concave curves impossible with CSS border-radius alone.

Lava Lamp Blobs

Soft gradient blobs rise and fall within a container, morphing shape as they move like viscous liquid in a lava lamp.

Water Ripple Effect

Concentric rings expand outward from a center point, fading as they grow to simulate the gentle disturbance of a water surface.

Gooey Metaballs

SVG blur and color matrix filters cause overlapping shapes to merge like liquid blobs combining under surface tension.

Melting Transitions

Shapes gradually deform downward as if melting, with border-radius shifting to create viscous, dripping visual effects.

Floating Blob Field

Multiple organic blobs drift independently at varying speeds, creating a layered atmospheric background of gentle motion.

Wavy Borders

Undulating edges replace hard lines, created via SVG paths and curved CSS shapes.
