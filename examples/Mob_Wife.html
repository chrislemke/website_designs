Design Principles

The rules of opulent maximalism

01

Command the Room

Every element should feel like it demands attention. Nothing whispers in Mob Wife design. Headlines are oversized, colors are deep, gold is generous.

02

Opulence Over Restraint

Layer textures, metallics, and dark richness. This is maximalism with purpose and power. Flat surfaces are lifeless here.

03

Dark Drama as Foundation

Begin with deep, moody backgrounds and build upward with gold and cream highlights. The darkness is not empty but velvet-rich.

04

Texture Is Everything

Every panel and background should have depth and materiality. Leopard print, velvet noise, and fur grain create the plush tactile world.

05

Gold Is the Signature

Treat gold accents the way a mob wife wears jewelry: generously but with intention. It appears in borders, dividers, headings, and buttons.

06

Confidence Without Apology

The design should feel powerful and self-assured, never tentative or understated. Italian glamour meets American excess in bold harmony.
