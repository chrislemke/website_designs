Woodland Enchantments

gentle magic for everyday tending

💧

Dewdrop Preservation

Collect morning dew from clover leaves before dawn breaks. Stored in glass vials sealed with beeswax, the dew retains its clarity for a full moon cycle and can be added to tinctures for gentle renewal.

🍄

Fern Spiral Compass

The unfurling direction of young fiddleheads in spring indicates the nearest source of fresh water. Follow the spiral inward, then trace the direction at its tightest coil to find hidden springs.

🧄

Mushroom Ring Etiquette

When encountering a fairy ring, walk three times clockwise around its perimeter before stepping inside. Leave dried lavender or a smooth river stone at the northern edge as thanks to the fae.

🌈 Fairy Wing Iridescence

This card demonstrates the iridescent shimmer border effect. The animated gradient cycles through moss green, dewdrop lilac, fairy wing pink, firefly gold, and enchanted teal, creating the appearance of light playing across a dragonfly wing. The effect uses CSS mask compositing with an animated background-position.

🌫 Morning Mist Panel

This frosted glass panel uses backdrop-filter: blur(16px) with a semi-transparent background to create the effect of peering through the early morning forest mist. When layered over textured backgrounds, it produces an atmospheric depth that is central to the Fairycore aesthetic.
