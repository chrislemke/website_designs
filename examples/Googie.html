Cards & Components

Signature Googie UI elements -- angled cards, neon signs, starburst badges, and chrome-plated buttons.

✶

Starburst Energy

Radiating spike patterns channel atomic optimism and cosmic wonder. The most iconic Googie ornament, evoking nuclear energy and the promise of an impossibly bright tomorrow.

➤

Boomerang Motion

Asymmetric V-curves sweep upward and outward, defying gravity and suggesting lift-off. The signature shape of Googie architecture appears as dividers, accents, and structural forms.

⚛

Atomic Optimism

Stylized electron orbits and molecular models reference the Atomic Age enthusiasm of the 1950s, when science promised a gleaming, effortless future for everyone.

★

Neon Boulevard

Bright neon tube outlines on signs and building edges, achieved through text-shadow and box-shadow glow techniques. The digital equivalent of a buzzing roadside marquee.
